# Privacy Policy for EasyDemo

**Last Updated: November 3, 2025**

## Introduction

EasyDemo ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how EasyDemo handles your information when you use our macOS screen recording application.

## Our Privacy Commitment

**EasyDemo is designed with privacy as a core principle.** The app operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

EasyDemo does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal identification information
- Usage analytics or telemetry data
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Device identifiers or hardware information
- Network activity or browsing history
- Location data
- Any information about your recordings or their content

## Information Stored Locally

EasyDemo stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

### Application Preferences
- Onboarding completion status (stored in macOS UserDefaults)
- User-configured settings for recordings (background styles, webcam preferences, etc.)

### Recording Files
- Video recordings are saved locally to your Movies folder (`~/Movies/EasyDemo/`)
- All recording data remains on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere
- You have full control over these files and can delete them at any time

## Permissions Required

EasyDemo requires the following macOS permissions to function:

### Screen Recording Permission
- **Purpose**: To capture the selected window or screen area you choose to record
- **Usage**: Only active during recording sessions
- **Control**: You can revoke this permission at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security > Screen Recording

### Camera Access
- **Purpose**: To add an optional webcam overlay to your recordings
- **Usage**: Only when you enable the webcam overlay feature
- **Control**: You can revoke this permission at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera

### Microphone Access
- **Purpose**: To record audio commentary alongside your screen recordings
- **Usage**: Only when you enable audio recording
- **Control**: You can revoke this permission at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone

## Data Processing

All video and audio processing occurs **entirely on your device** using Apple's native frameworks:
- ScreenCaptureKit for screen capture
- AVFoundation for video encoding
- Core Image for visual effects

No data is sent to external servers or cloud services.

## Third-Party Services

EasyDemo does **NOT** use any third-party services, including:
- Analytics platforms
- Crash reporting tools
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage providers
- Social media integrations

## Data Retention

- Application preferences remain on your device until you uninstall the app
- Recording files remain in your Movies folder until you manually delete them
- Uninstalling EasyDemo will remove application preferences but will not delete your recording files

## Children's Privacy

EasyDemo does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Data Security

Your recordings and preferences are stored using macOS's standard security mechanisms:
- Files are protected by your macOS user account permissions
- Data never leaves your device
- No encryption keys or passwords are stored by the app

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: All data is stored in standard macOS locations you can access
- **Deletion**: Simply delete the app to remove preferences, or delete recording files manually
- **Portability**: Recording files are in standard .mov format and can be moved or copied freely
- **Permission Control**: Revoke any permission through System Settings at any time

## Compliance

EasyDemo is designed to comply with:
- Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
- macOS Privacy Best Practices
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles

Since the app does not collect personal data, most data protection regulations do not apply to EasyDemo's operation.
